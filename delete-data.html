Data Deletion Request

Campus Meal does not store user data on its own servers. All personal preferences (favorites, name, etc.) are stored locally on your device.

How to Delete Your Data:

  • In-App: Go to Settings and tap "Reset App". This will permanently delete all local data.
  • Contact: You can also request data deletion or ask questions by emailing us at: kaiwen.info@gmail.com

Once you delete the app or use the Reset button, we no longer have access to any of your local preferences.
